Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

About

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is independent film festival being held in Brooklyn, New York. Starting on August 5th, it will last until August 8th. The movies will be playing from 6:00PM all the way up to midnight. There should be enough time for everyon to catch a movie, sit back and relax! The event is organized for film lovers of all ages, showing family-friendly movies as well as cult classics and some of the best from horror genre.
Tickets are entirely free, but you must secure your spot by registering on our website!
